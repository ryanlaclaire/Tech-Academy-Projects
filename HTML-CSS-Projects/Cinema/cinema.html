About Academy Cinemas

Located in the beautiful city of Portland, Oregon's Pearl District!

Our reels have been spinning since 1959. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles is our pleasure.

Accomodations

All of our auditoriums are equipped with brad new Christie 3D® sound systems!

Relax in our extra wide, plush cushioning recliners. Seating is on a first-come-first-served basis. Our seating arrangment is disability friendly♿.

Our auditoriums are climate controlled to approximately 72° year around.
